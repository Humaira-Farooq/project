Persol vs. Ray-Ban:


RAY-BAN

Ray-Ban glasses, on the other hand, are known for their iconic style and versatility. They offer a wide range of frames, from classic aviators to bold Wayfarers and trendy Clubmasters. Ray-Ban glasses are often made from lightweight materials such as nylon and metal, and they offer a mix of polarized and non-polarized lenses. Ray-Ban glasses tend to have a more casual and sporty look, and they are often favored by those who want to make a fashion statement.

PERSOL

Persol glasses are known for their sophisticated Italian design and high-quality materials, such as acetate and metal. They are often recognized by their signature arrow hinge and polarized lenses. Persol glasses tend to have a more classic and elegant look, and they are often favored by those who appreciate fine craftsmanship and attention to detail..
